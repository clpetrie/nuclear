\subsection*{1.1}
Start by adding and subracting three terms to the commutation relation.
\begin{align}
   [A&B,CD] = ABCD-CDAB \\
      &= ABCD-CDAB + ACBD-ACBD + CADB-CADB + ACDB-ACDB \\
      &= ABCD+ACBD - ACBD-ACDB - CDAB-CADB + CADB+ACDB \\
      &= A{C,B}D - AC{D,B} - C{D,A}B + {CA}DB
\end{align}
